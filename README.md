Airline High Cloud Analysis Project 

Tools & Technologies Used - Power Bi | SQL | Tableau | Advance Excel

𝐏𝐫𝐨𝐣𝐞𝐜𝐭 𝐎𝐛𝐣𝐞𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐯𝐞:
 
This project aims to track key performance metrics like trip Top Performing Airlines by Passenger Volume, Load Factor Variability Across Airlines, Route Efficiency, Load Factor Balance Total Passengers, Total available Seats and Total Departure. The analysis helps improve Passenger retention, restructure low-demand routes, and introducing seasonal pricing strategies for growth and enhanced passenger experience.

𝐊𝐞𝐲 𝐈𝐧𝐬𝐢𝐠𝐡𝐭𝐬:

1.Total Passengers = 187.02M and Total Available Seats = 244M
 → ~57M seats remain unfilled.
2.Total Departures: 2.80M - Departures are significantly higher than flights
3. Yearly / Monthly and Quarterly Load Factor shows Load factor tends to rise year-over-year if demand grows faster than capacity.
4.Q2 and Q4 and December, June, July often show higher load factors due to summer holidays and year-end travel.
5. Highest load factor: Globe span Airways (~94.76%) and top passengers: Southwest & Delta 
6. Few routes dominate (e.g., Chicago–Detroit, Washington–Boston) 
7. Flights <500 miles = 58,047 (very high) and Flights >3000 miles = significantly low
8. Southwest Airlines Co. is the highest preferred carrier with 336,498 departures and Delta Air Lines Inc. is second with 217,702 departures. 

𝐑𝐞𝐜𝐨𝐦𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐝𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧𝐬:
1. Airlines Reduce the Gap Between Available Seats And Actual Passengers - Offer Last-minute Deals & Loyalty Rewards For Unsold Inventory
-Implement Dynamic Pricing Models To Fill Empty Seats
2. Airlines Can Balance Short-haul And Long-haul Operations by Increase Long-haul Route Investment Where Demand Exists. 
3. Airlines Can Achieve Higher Efficiency Despite Lower Passenger Volumes by Improve Route Optimization And Turnaround Time.
4. Airlines optimize route networks to maximize flight utilization by Reduce or restructure low-demand routes and Use hub-and-spoke models to consolidate passengers efficiently.
5. Lower-performing airlines can improve by Reduce underutilized routes and focus on high-demand sectors 
6. Airline industry can improve overall passenger traffic by Increasing partnerships or code-sharing with high-performing airlines .
